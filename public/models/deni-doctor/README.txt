DENI / DOCTOR CHARACTER ASSET — VEO ZAVOD

FILES
- deni-doctor-rigged.glb: browser-ready UV humanoid with a 56-bone full-finger skeleton and restrained idle clip.
- deni-doctor-mixamo.obj: neutral A-pose source for Mixamo auto-rigging.
- deni-doctor.mtl + textures/: material package for the OBJ.
- THIRD_PARTY.txt: CC0 base-mesh provenance.

SCALE AND AXES
- Real-world scale, approximately 1.83 m.
- Y up, Z forward.
- Origin at ground centre.

MIXAMO ROUTE
1. Upload the provided ZIP or the OBJ together with its MTL and textures.
2. Place the chin, wrists, elbows, knees and groin markers.
3. Use the finger skeleton route if the hand recognition succeeds; otherwise the no-fingers route remains safe.
4. Export FBX for Unity with skin.

The GLB preserves the CC0 body's continuous skinned topology and adds Deni's coat, sleeves, pockets, hair, face details and material breakdown. Coat sections are production blockout geometry and should receive a final deformation pass before close-up animation.
