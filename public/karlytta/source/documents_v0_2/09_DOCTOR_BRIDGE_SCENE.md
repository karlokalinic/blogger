# Playable Scene: The Doctor and the Access Log

## Function

This remains the only required bridge between Karlytta and the main game. It must work for a player who ignores the archive.

## Location

Night records office below the clinic. Heat is uneven. One fluorescent tube starts several seconds after the others. A waiting-room television is audible through a ventilation duct, carrying NARODNA KUĆA under static.

## Task

The Doctor needs an earlier laboratory result for a patient whose current sample may be mislabeled. The main archive is partly offline, so he uses a local audit terminal.

## Discovery

The file shows seven accesses before the appointment, two after discharge, one RP Network service account and one access under the Doctor’s own credentials at a time he cannot remember being present.

The patient name is damaged or hidden. An optional archive reader may recognize the patient identifier.

## Choices

### Report immediately

The file freezes pending compliance review, delaying treatment.

`REPORTED UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS`

### Copy the result, then report

The Doctor obtains useful information and creates another unauthorized copy.

`USED THE BREACH TO TREAT`

### Delete the RP entry

He protects privacy or destroys evidence.

`ALTERED THE AUDIT TRAIL`

### Ignore it

Fastest path. Later access expands.

`TREATED THE FILE AS NOISE`

## Dialogue

**COLLEAGUE:** We need the result, not the history of everybody who looked at it.

**DOCTOR:** The history changes what the result is.

**COLLEAGUE:** No. It changes what it costs.

Never reveal in this scene whether the record belongs to Karlytta. Ethics cannot depend on celebrity value.
