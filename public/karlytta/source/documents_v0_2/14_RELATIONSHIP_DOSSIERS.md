# Karlytta: Relationship Dossiers

## Ognjen Varda

Publicly treats her as proof that national glamour can coexist with tradition. The complaint describes access treated as gratitude owed. Their relationship is made of strategically spaced encounters, invitations, absences and institutional consequences rather than constant intimacy.

## Sonja Dravac

Can calm Karlytta on camera better than almost anyone. This is partly care and partly professional extraction. Karlytta trusts Sonja’s timing and distrusts everything the timing serves.

## Dorian Belić

Understands her instincts and converts them into commercially manageable form. He often improves the songs. He also decides which discomfort remains useful.

## Marta Orel

Mutual admiration with class resentment. Marta envies reach. Karlytta envies automatic seriousness. Tender privately, cautious publicly.

## Nika Rime

A younger artist who grew up copying Karlytta and is marketed as her replacement. Karlytta alternates between mentorship, cruelty and recognition.

## Mina Plevnik

Karlytta depends on Mina’s archive and finds it invasive. Mina preserves deleted material Karlytta sometimes wants gone. Their conflict asks whether a fan protects someone by refusing their request to forget.

## Lea Pažić

Protects the legal case from Karlytta’s need to transform every fact into a public sentence. Karlytta calls her cold. Lea considers that evidence she is doing her job.

## The Doctor

They do not need to meet. Their structural relationship is enough: both work through bodies, want expertise to outrun death, confuse controlled language with safety and face records that know more than their creators.
