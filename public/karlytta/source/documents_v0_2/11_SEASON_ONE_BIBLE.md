# Karlytta Archive: Season One Bible

## Episode 1 — EVERYTHING WAS OUTSIDE

Complaint filed. Official track returns. The country quotes one statement differently.

## Episode 2 — THE VERSION YOU KNOW WAS SHORTENED

NOĆNI ZAPIS reappears. Ownership notices conflict. An old press kit proves the longer export was known internally.

## Episode 3 — WE WILL NOT SPECULATE

NARODNA KUĆA performs responsible non-speculation. Paid voting exposes how participation is manufactured.

## Episode 4 — ELEVEN MISSING MINUTES

Raw 2013 footage emerges. Karlytta remains volatile, but the cause and sequence of reaction change.

## Episode 5 — PROTECTIVE ACCESS

Clinic logs show non-clinical access affecting more than one patient. The story stops being celebrity exceptionalism.

## Episode 6 — FORT

Rehearsal material from FORT UMBRALIS returns. The most dangerous line was written by another person and preserved because Karlytta refused to remove it.

## Episode 7 — THE WITNESS WITH A JOB

Sven prepares to testify and loses employment through an apparently unrelated restructuring. He is also revealed to have sold minor archive material years earlier.

## Episode 8 — NATIONAL DAUGHTER

Internal documents trace the label’s mandatory use. Karlytta herself approved one early charity campaign using it.

## Episode 9 — THE COURT DOES NOT LISTEN TO CHORUSES

The court rejects simplistic lyrical proof and orders broad preservation. Law is a partial tool rather than savior or sham.

## Episode 10 — ACCESS LOG

The Doctor encounters the audit anomaly. His action changes whether evidence survives but does not decide the lawsuit.

## Ending

Karlytta performs the official track. At the low response, the backing stops. She remains silent. The crowd supplies the line. No speech follows.
