# KARLYTTA: Career Résumé and Discography

## 2003–2005: Municipal visibility

Youth programmes, civic ceremonies, shopping-centre stages and winter broadcasts. **PRVA** succeeds because it can mean romantic ambition, professional declaration or an insult directed at everyone placed before her.

## 2006–2009: National manufacture

A multi-party contract joins RP Network, the Varda Cultural Foundation and a private label. Metallic fabrics, liturgical silhouettes, synthetic glamour and volatile interviews become her durable grammar. The restricted 2008 performance central to the lawsuit occurs here.

## 2010–2013: Total visibility

Every haircut, argument, endorsement and cancellation becomes public memory. **FRIENDLY STAKES**, staged around a poker table, is mocked as celebrity self-seriousness and later rediscovered as a work about unequal risk. A seventeen-minute 2013 interview is cut to six, producing the canonical image of her instability.

## 2014–2016: Self-title and institutional conflict

**KARLYTTA** is released. A longer file later called **NOĆNI ZAPIS** circulates anonymously. **FORT UMBRALIS**, publicly funded and symphonic, premieres once and disappears under “technical revision.”

## 2017–2019: Respectability crisis

**MEMORIA MARIS** wins elite recognition. Karlytta resents praise implying an orchestra finally made her intelligent.

## 2020–2022: Third War and emergency use

**ZRAKOPERKA** becomes continuity music for evacuation notices and later recruitment imagery. She walks out of **NARODNA KUĆA** in 2022 after being asked whether trauma improves her commercial value.

## 2023–2026: Body, archive and litigation

The **ANESTEZIJA** era foregrounds procedures, clocks, bodies, signals and consent. In 2026, she files civil action.
