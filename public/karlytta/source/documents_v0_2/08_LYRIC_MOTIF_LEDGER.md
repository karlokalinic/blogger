# KARLYTTA Lyric Motif Ledger

## Multiplication

Copies, mirrors, crowds, names and “one hundred.” Surface: celebrity abundance. Private pressure: one body can be erased. Political pressure: symbols should be infinitely reproducible. Doctor echo: duplicated patient numbers. Do not turn this into literal cloning.

## Floors and ascent

Stairs, lifts, towers, stages and mountains. Surface: ambition. Private pressure: motion used to avoid stillness. Political pressure: hierarchy disguised as progress. Doctor echo: wards and triage. At least one descent must feel like relief.

## Signals

Radio, phones, pulse, subtitles, alarms and dropped connections. Surface: modern isolation. Private pressure: speech arriving as the wrong category. Political pressure: control of transmission. Doctor echo: monitors and alerts. Sometimes the equipment is simply broken.

## Contracts and clocks

Signatures, hourly life, invoices, appointments and countdowns. Surface: work satire. Private pressure: consent converted into continued availability. Political pressure: domination made administratively clean. Doctor echo: shifts and forms signed late. Some contracts must genuinely protect.

## Body as document

Throat, skin, feet, nails, breath, blood, scars and medication. Surface: sensuality. Private pressure: the body retaining what language delays. Political pressure: bodily truth matters only after institutional certification. Doctor echo: diagnosis and privacy. Suffering is not automatically accurate.

## Child language

Broken grammar, diminutives, counting and game rules. Surface: camp or flirtation. Private pressure: refusal denied adult status. Political pressure: authority decides which voice sounds competent. Karlytta must also possess devastating adult precision.

## Repetition

Hooks, ticks, confirmations and names. Surface: pop efficiency. Private pressure: conditioning and memory. Political pressure: consensus produced through frequency. Every repeated phrase should change speaker, setting or cost.
