# RP NETWORK / BREAKING

## Karlytta files civil action against President Varda, Presidency and RP Network

**PRUDINA, 16 July 2026, 19:40**

Singer and public figure Karlytta filed a civil complaint this afternoon against President Ognjen Varda, the Office of the President, RP Network and the Varda Cultural Foundation.

The filing alleges sexual coercion following a restricted state performance in 2008 and describes a subsequent pattern of professional pressure, surveillance, unauthorized access to private records, misuse of her voice and image, and retaliatory media treatment. The defendants deny wrongdoing.

The public version of the complaint is forty-three pages long. Several exhibits, including medical material, travel records and studio documentation, remain sealed. Karlytta’s attorney Lea Pažić said the case concerns “specific acts by identifiable people” and asked commentators not to substitute lyric interpretation for evidence.

The Office of the President called the complaint “a politically timed reconstruction of a long public partnership.” It did not answer whether President Varda met Karlytta privately after the 2008 performance identified in the filing.

RP Network has initiated an internal review of archival access and editorial procedure. The network rejects the claim that entertainment footage was edited to conceal distress and notes that Karlytta repeatedly returned to RP programming over the following years.

Shortly before the filing became public, an older extended version of the self-titled track KARLYTTA began circulating again. Its authenticity has not been established. Three previous removal notices attributed ownership to three different entities.

Karlytta did not take questions. As she entered the court building, she stopped once and said:

> Pisala sam to. Govorila sam to. Pjevala sam to cijeli život. Sve je bilo vani. Samo nitko nije slušao.

RP Network will not publish sealed medical material or unverified allegations concerning private persons. A special edition of NARODNA KUĆA begins at 21:00 under the title **WE WILL NOT SPECULATE**.
